### Boxer Gear Safety Checklist
*   **Check the Brisket Strap**: Ensure it sits 2 inches behind the armpits to prevent "chafing" during high-speed running.
*   **The 2-Finger Rule**: You should be able to fit two fingers under every strap. If it’s tighter, it restricts the Boxer's lung expansion—a major issue for this athletic breed.
*   **Front-Clip for Training**: Use a front-leash attachment to redirect the Boxer's vertical momentum into a lateral turn.
