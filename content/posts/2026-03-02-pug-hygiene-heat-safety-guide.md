### Pug Health Maintenance Checklist
1. **Wipe the Rope Daily**: Use a clean wipe for the nose fold every single night.
2. **Weight Management**: A 2lb weight gain in a Pug is equivalent to a 20lb gain in a human—this directly impacts their ability to breathe.
3. **The "AC Rule"**: If it’s too hot for you to sit comfortably outside without a fan, it’s too hot for your Pug.
